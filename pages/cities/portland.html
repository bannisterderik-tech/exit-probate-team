The market

What Portland probate looks like in practice.

Portland, Oregon sits in Multnomah County and is administered through the Multnomah County Circuit Court when probate is opened locally. The estate mix in Portland reflects the largest probate dockets in the state — and the right strategy for a property here depends on whether the heirs want speed, price, or both.

Multnomah, Washington, Clackamas, and the corridor cities together carry roughly half of Oregon's probate filings every year. Property values are high, family geography is wide, and timelines are tight. Most files cross county lines before they close.

For a city of roughly 652,503 residents, Portland produces a steady volume of probate property files every year, and the gap between the best and worst-handled cases is enormous. The same protocol that we run statewide applies here — sharpened to Portland's pricing dynamics and timeline pressure.
